Innovation camp – “micro plastic”

Wed, des 2, 2020 2 AM

St. Halvorsgate, Bergen, Norge

Research shows micro-plastic is everywhere. In the nature, in our food and even on Antarctica where almost no humans live. The micro-plastic ends in nature through different channels. For instance, through dumped waste in the nature, by washing synthetic clothes and from car wheels. Join in on this innovation camp to try solve some of the challenges with micro plastic!
