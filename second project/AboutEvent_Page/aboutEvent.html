10 NOV. KL. 13:00 UTC+01 - 12 NOV. KL. 18:00

INOVATION CAMP
- "How can Oslo be better prepared for winter?"

Deichmanske bibliotek

Buy ticket here

Welcome to this years biggest innovation event, with a winner price at 20 000 NOK.
As always with INOVA, the innovation camps are based upon frugal innovation. This year the challenge is to find solutions for Oslo to become better prepared for winter.
Today, when the first snow arrives in the city there is always chaotic. Car crashes, it is hard to get the snow out of the streets and people get hurt falling on the ice. Does it have to be like this? Can Oslo become a city that is well prepared for winter? We belive you have the answer!

Come join us making the impossible possible!
